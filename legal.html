Risk Disclaimer — read this one

Alphalumina is not financial advice. It is research and measurement software. Nothing on this site, in the platform, in any brief, alert, receipt, score, or grade is a recommendation, solicitation, or offer to buy or sell any asset.

  • Meme-coins are among the highest-risk assets in existence. In our own measured data, roughly 85% of newly launched tokens die, and the majority of "opportunities" round-trip to near-zero. Assume any position can go to zero, quickly.
  • Grades and validation results are historical measurements, produced after the fact. Past outcomes — including honestly graded ones — do not predict future results.
  • Paper-validation results are simulated. Simulations cannot fully capture slippage, failed transactions, MEV, network congestion, or the behavior of markets under stress, even when we model fill costs deliberately.
  • Our own published measurements show that our scoring engine currently has no proven predictive edge for returns; it functions as a risk filter. We publish that because it is true.
  • You are solely responsible for your decisions. If you trade, use capital you can afford to lose entirely, and consider consulting a licensed financial advisor. We are not one.
