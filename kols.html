The deeper problem

Track records don't persist.

We tested whether a KOL's past performance predicts their future performance — classified each wallet on its early trades, measured it on later ones (no overlap, 2,356 vs 2,683 buys, as of 2026-07-06). Wallets with good track records hit 2x on 42.6% of future buys; wallets with bad ones, 36.8%. Within noise. Past performance did not predict the future — so even this page's top table is a history, not a forecast. That's the honest limit of every leaderboard, including ours.

What we can't see: these records cover the wallets publicly linked to each KOL. Many KOLs trade from undisclosed side wallets that are not visible to us — or to anyone. A KOL who looks unprofitable here may be profitable on hidden wallets, and vice versa. Everything on this page is based only on the information we can actually gather and verify; we'd rather show you that limit than pretend it isn't there.
